In 1974, RCI pioneered the concept of exchange holidays. Since then, millions of owners have discovered that an RCI subscribing membership really enhances the quality of their holiday ownership.

Today, RCI has more than three million members worldwide, who enjoy dream holidays at over 3,700 of RCI's affiliated resorts. These Holiday ownership members and affiliated resorts are a unique community of travel enthusiasts with the common bond of a lifetime commitment to quality holidays through Holiday ownership. RCI members benefit from the knowledge, experience and resources of RCI's guides, who provide assistance in the planning of exchange holidays.

An exchange holiday allows RCI members to discover new places and enjoy the freedom of travelling all over the world. RCI is considered within the holiday ownership industry as the global leader in exchange holidays.

RCI has been at the forefront of the holiday ownership industry for over 30 years, maintaining the highest standards of quality and excellence as a growth partner to its resort affiliates. It is also part of Wyndham Worldwide, one of the largest hospitality companies across six continents with a premier portfolio of world renowned brands.

Wyndham Worldwide is one of the world's leading diversified providers of travel-related products and services for businesses and individual consumers, with world renowned brands in lodging franchising, vacation ownership, vacation rentals and vacation exchange.

It is also the world’s largest lodging franchisor, encompassing nearly 6,500 hotels under 10 brands on six continents.
