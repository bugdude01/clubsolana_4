Welcome Week

Booking your Welcome Week

New members are offered the opportunity to take advantage of a complimentary week providing the holiday is booked within three months of completion of your membership, the offer is valid for a full twelve months from your date of purchase so do not delay any longer, your welcome week can be taken at anytime of the year subject to availability and irrespective of your season owned, to book your holiday or for further information please contact our promotions department on promotions@clubsolana.com or by telephone on 00356 21520117 during normal office hours.

Terms and conditions:

Only members and joint members can take up the offer.

Members must take a “new member presentation”

Offer is not valid for travel or transportation to and from the airport

Half Board is included, however the current utility fee applies

Offer is subject to availability, whilst every opportunity will be made to secure club suites for members we cannot guarantee their occupancy, the club reserves the right to offer you a hotel room as a alternative.

Welcome week’s booked out with the three month booking window are subject to a charge of €49.00 per person per week.
