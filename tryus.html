Try Us!

 

Give us a week, and we'll give you the WORLD

With our trial programme, you can experience the kind of quality that all our members have come to expect since joining us. Why not take a week in the sun to relax, unwind and discover the WORLDWIDE possibilities that await you.

To qualify for this programme you must meet the following requirements:

  • Participants must be minimum 30 years of age, be married or living together for a period of minimum 3 years and one of the party must be in a full-time employment.
  • Your stay at our resort is at a reduced fee, but if the presentation is not attended by both parties, the full rental fee will be charged.
  • Your written confirmation must be brought along to show upon arrival.
  • The holiday accomodation is provided for a maximum of 4 people.
  • The Club Solana reserves the right to accomodate you in other resorts than ours, depending on availability.

The offer is not open to current Club Solana/Pergola members. This is a one time offer only. The only obligation of this offer is that you spend a morning of your holiday time with us, where we will introduce you to our unique Holiday - Ownership concept. In a no obligation one-to-one tour we will simply point out the many benefits of Holiday - Ownership and explain why thousands of member families allow RCI to look after their holiday needs every year.

Please note that the sending of the accommodation request is not a confirmation of reservation. Your application will only be valid and binding once you receive a written response from the reservations department. At this point the accommodation fee will have been settled in full. Once a reservation has been confirmed there will be no refund in the event of cancellation. To ensure that flights can be matched to your accommodation we will issue a temporary confirmation valid for 3 working days to cover any amendments or cancellation of the reservation. If a cancellation is not received by the close of this period then your credit card will be charged with the full reservation cost.
