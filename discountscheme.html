Club Card

 

As a member of Club Solana, you also receive the Member Discount Card. This card entitles you to discounts on Food and Drinks at both the Solana and Pergola as well as Discounts from "Herbies" jewellery store in Mellieha.

Other venues which give discounts to club members are being added all the time so watch this space!

Current discounts are as follows:

Solana & Pergola Hotels:

  • Food - 10%
  • Drinks - 5%

Herbies Jewellery:

  • 10% Discount on all purchases.
