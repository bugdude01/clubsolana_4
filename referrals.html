Referral Programme

Our top class resorts are located at one of the most beautiful spots on the Maltese Islands. The Melliha area on Malta's north coast offers a perfect holiday setting. A clean golden sand beach, all kinds of leisure facilities and one of the best restaurant selections on the Island ensure that your stay with us is relaxing and pleasurable

The Pergola and Solana Resorts are designed to make your holiday a memorable one. Stylish decoration, spacious rooms and modern equipment give a touch of luxury to your accommodation. From your private balcony, you have amazing views over the church, and can see as far as the neighbouring islands of Gozo and Comino.

Enjoy your luxury vacations in a friendly club atmosphere, where you won't be treated just like another tourist but as a member of our club family. All the staff at Pergola & Solana will be happy to help you with any advice or service you might need to make your holiday the best it should be.

We are looking forward to welcoming you soon as one of our exclusive guests.
