Med Jen

Medjen is the entity that we use at Club Solana to facilitate maintenance fees, banking allocations and other general queries regarding your membership. The lady responsible for this is Ann Jennings. Some information from Ann herself is detailed below:

 

My name is Ann Jennings and I am currently the Chairperson of the owner’s Committee at Pergola and have been so since 1989.  We have been owners since 1987.

I am married to Chris – who many of you may know as our Minute Secretary – we have two children (well adults now!).  When I first became involved with the Pergola I was teaching full time college students, after this I moved on to work for a national charity as Head of F.E. and helping to set up a new centre here in the Midlands; after this I continued my career in teaching/training and finally become Education and training Manager for a national company.  It was at this point my work and involvement with Pergola was increasing and I made a conscious decision to become more involved with Pergola and to do this on a full time basis incorporating Medjen as the collecting agents for maintenance fees.   This I have now been doing for the last 11 years.

In September 2007 I took over as collecting Agent for the Solana Hotel too.  In my role here I deal with all maintenance fee issues; general day to day queries/problems; your banking allocations; hopefully keep you all up to  date with what’s going on at Solana …
