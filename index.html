Welcome!

Welcome to the official website of Club Solana - Malta's premier vacation club in the beautiful village of Mellieha.

Please take the time to have a good look around the site and see exactly what being a member of Club Solana has to offer. Also, do make sure to check in regularly for updates on what's on in Malta and developments at the resort and with the club in general.

We are always looking for contributions from our members as well, so please do feel free to get in touch with us if you have any feedback, or would like to share your holiday experiences with us. This can be with the Solana Hotel itself, or indeed with any of the other many thousands of resorts that you have access to as a member of Club Solana.

If you are here for the first time and are NOT a member, we would love to hear from you as well. Maybe you would be interested in visiting us and seeing what we have to offer?

Either way, we are looking forward to welcoming you soon as one of our exclusive guests.
