Who are we ?

FNTC is part of IFG Group Plc, a Dublin based financial services company quoted on both the London and Dublin stock exchanges. FNTC is the market leader in providing consumer protection and support services to the holiday ownership industry. The leading specialist holiday ownership trustee company in the global industry, FNTC protects the interests of Club Solana Members by acting as trustee for all Solana Club apartments. Other companies using FNTC's services as trustees include Marriott, RCI and Hilton Grand Vacation Club. FNTC has more than 15,000 Holiday ownership units in trust worldwide.

Our role ?

All payments are made in favour of a third party (FNTC) therefore, ensuring that once the contract of sale is complete the client receives their certificate of ownership in the specific season and size as detailed on the purchase agreement allowing the new member complete peace of mind. FNTC ensure that all the elements of the contract are fulfilled, therefore, providing full and comprehensive consumer protection within the European Union.
