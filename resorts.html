Resort

 

Location

The Solana Hotel is ideally positioned right in the heart of the picturesque village of Mellieha, which has a great variety of bars, restaurants and shops. With a population of around 5,000 friendly locals it is renowned for it’s fantastic panoramic views. Mellieha also has the largest sandy beach in Malta situated just 1 kilometre from the village core! These characteristics make Mellieha the perfect place for locals and visitors all year round.

Apartments

Club Solana members have access to either Studio of Sleep-Four apartments giving complete privacy for either two or four persons respectively. All suites are fully equipped with Satelite TV, Sofa Bed, Dining facilities and the innovative compact kitchenette

All Club members have His & Hers bathrobes, Fruit Basket, Bottle of wine and additional toiletries.

Room facilities include En-suite luxury bathroom with haidryer, Satellite TV, Direct Dial Telephone, ISDN laptop connection, individually controlled air-conditioning, radio alarm clock, frigo bar, tea & coffee making facilities, and either a terrace or balcony.

Amenities

The Solana consists of 70 units over 5 floors serviced with 2 lifts. There is also the Tosca Restaurant, Cafe Puccini, Bar, Rooftop Pool Bar, Multi-Purpose Hall, Wi-Fi Facilities, Internet Cafe, Games Room & TV Area and a 24-Hour Reception.

Leisure Facilities

Indoor Heated Pool, Jacuzzi, Fitness Room, Roof-Top Hydro Massage Pool and wonderful Sun Terraces. Also coming soon to the resort will be the "Monique Spa Garden", where you can pamper yourself in one of the three treatment rooms, steam away your stress in the Sauna or enjoy many (or all) of the wonderful massage and relaxation therapies on offer.
